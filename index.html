Hummingbird Labs

We make immersive technologies for everyday experiences.

And they do not subtract your attention from the real world.

Hummingbird Labs is an amalgamation of technology and arts. We are a bunch smitten with the idea of arts as a way of life, and technology as a method of creation.

With mixed reality and robotics, we imagine a world where immersive technologies augment our lives, instead of creating a parallel digital one.
So that's what we do at Hummingbird Labs – make immersive technologies running in the background and enhancing the way we interpret our physical world.
